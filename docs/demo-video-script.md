# Demo Video Script

Target length: 2 to 3 minutes

## Opening

Clear-Transit helps dispatch operators catch risky intra-city shipments early, understand why a delay is likely, and compare a reroute before the delivery slips.

## Flow

1. Open the live MVP link.
2. Show the login page.
3. Register or log in.
4. Land on the dashboard and introduce the map, KPI strip, conditions panel, shipment watchlist, and alerts feed.
5. Focus on one active shipment and explain the current ETA and risk indicators.
6. Trigger `Refresh` to show updated monitoring conditions.
7. Highlight the route explanation and the reroute recommendation.
8. Show the before-and-after ETA comparison.
9. Close by restating the impact: faster, clearer reroute decisions for intra-city dispatch teams.

## Delivery Notes

- Record from the hosted URL, not localhost.
- Keep the narration tight and factual.
- Avoid promising features that are not visible in the MVP.
- End with the public GitHub repo and deck link ready in the submission form.
